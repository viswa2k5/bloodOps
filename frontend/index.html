Frequently Asked Questions

Most healthy adults aged 18–65 weighing at least 50kg can donate. You should not have donated in the last 3 months and must be free from infections at the time of donation.
Whole blood can be donated every 3 months (90 days). Your last donation date is tracked in BloodOps to keep you safe.
Your Donor ID is a unique identifier assigned when you register. Use it to check your donation history, download certificates, and track your contributions over time.
BloodOps uses ABO and Rh compatibility rules to match donors to hospital requests. O- is the universal donor, while AB+ can receive from all types. Click the compatibility chart button for a full overview.
